Joining SP

I came into SP DAAA course via Early Admission Exercise (EAE) as programming, tinkering with computers & learning AI is my passion. Even though I have only been in SP for a single term, I already have had a lot of new & exciting experiences and made a bunch of lovely friends.

Experience in SP SoC

Orientation

Orientation was quite fun. We got to make new friends and bond with our future classmatess. Though, we had to sit through hours of Welcome talks and policy briefings in a freezing theatre.

Programming lessons

Programming lessons were very interesting but content heavy at the same time. We learnt how to program in Javascript and make websites using HTML and CSS. During computing lessons, we also learnt about the very basics of how computers work.

Project Skill lessons

I have learnt useful team skills such as presentation skills as well as effective teamwork communication skills. These skills could easily utilized in any work setting I am in in the future.

Food in SP

SP has a wide variety of food to choose from. Some of my favourite food courts are Food Court 3 & 5 as they have the tastiest food. Food Court 4 is an excellent balance between comfort, convenience and food quality.

COVID-19

The pandamic has definitely taken a huge toll on our lives. In fact, one of my good friends have been infected with the virus. However, in SP, we are prepared and are doing full Home Based Learning and given free swab tests.
